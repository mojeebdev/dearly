## What is Dearly?

Dearly is a precision emotional intelligence engine built to eliminate the "writer's block" of the heart.

You tell it who they are. It tells you what to say.

Using 4 unique persona-driven tones — including **Dearly** (Sincere), **Romantic**, **Funny**, and **Inspirational**; Dearly maps your relationship and specific details to move beyond generic "Happy Birthday" or "Happy Valentine's" texts.

No clichés. No overthinking. Just **Beautiful Personal Greetings**.

---

## The Problem

Every year, millions of people struggle to express genuine emotion through text. We settle for dry, copied-and-pasted messages because finding the right words takes too much mental energy. "How do I tell them I care?" shouldn't be a source of stress.

Dearly, i see you. We kill that stress entirely.

## How It Works

1. **Pick your tone**: How do you want to sound? Sincere? Poetic? Hilarious?
2. **Enter the context**: Tell the Guardian their name, their hobbies, and why they matter to you.
3. **Get your message**: Dearly returns a precision-crafted, heartfelt message tailored to your recipient and the occasion.
